About Our Store

Coco's Chocolatiers is in no way affiliated with Disney/Pixar's new film, Coco.

Trust me, if it was, this small store would be making a lot more money than it does currently.

Our store was founded in 1868 by a poor man who had a passion for chocolate. What started out as a poor family business expanded into one of the most successful chocolate retail stores across the continent.

Our corporate headquarters is located in Arcadia Bay, Oregon, United States. However, there are 250+ locations scattered about the United States and Canada, and a few in Mexico. You can use our location map to find the store closest to you:
